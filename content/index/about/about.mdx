Mandy Rose, a retired educator, grandmother, mother and wife to Alvin, an ex-marine. She has two sons Duran and Darien. Mandy and Alvin are seasoned travelers with a wanderlust and drive for adventure.

Mandy has always been passionate about reading and writing. She enjoys telling stories and inspiring others and believes by sharing her stories and experiences she can help others through a personal story, a powerful quote or her her wildest imagination. She is always looking for ways to inspire, motivate and encourage others to pursue their dreams and live their best lives. She is excited to share her book 'BELONGED' a story of love, betrayal, faith and hope.
