Oozing with charisma, irresistible, charming, dapper, and brilliant, just about sums up Dominique Alexander. Amber is smitten. 'I need you here, Amber. I've never felt like this before.' Amber felt the same. Leaving her country, family, career and home she follows her heart. They languish in their perfect love for each other. Just when everything is splendidly foolproof, Dominique disappears. Isolated, heartbroken, embarrassed an petrified in a strange country, Amber is in a quandary. Amber vows to never love again.
